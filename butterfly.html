Wake, butterfly—

Wake, butterfly—
it's late, we've miles
to go together.

— Matsuo Bashō

Visualization Notes

This visualization interprets Bashō's haiku through a delicate particle system that embodies the butterfly's awakening and journey:

Visual Elements:

  • Wing Particles (White): Form the butterfly's wings, gently fluttering to create a sense of awakening
  • Trail Particles (Light Blue): Create an ethereal trail behind the butterfly, representing the journey
  • Sparkle Particles (Soft Yellow): Add moments of light and energy, suggesting the morning's awakening

Interactive Features:

  • Move your mouse to interact with the butterfly, creating gentle disturbances in its flight
  • The butterfly's wings flutter continuously, creating a sense of life and movement
  • Sparkles appear occasionally, adding moments of brightness to the journey
  • The subtle gradient background suggests the passage of time and space

Visual Metaphors:

  • The butterfly's gentle movement represents the awakening described in the haiku
  • The trailing particles suggest the "miles to go" mentioned in the poem
  • The interactive nature creates a sense of companionship between viewer and butterfly
  • The ethereal quality of the particles reflects the delicate nature of both the butterfly and the haiku form

Controls:

  • Move mouse to interact with the butterfly
  • Press 'p' to pause/resume the animation
  • Press 'r' to reset the visualization
  • Press 'i' to toggle these instructions

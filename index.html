About the Project

Synesthesia is an exploration of the intersection between poetry, emotion, and interactive visualization. Each sketch in this collection offers a unique way to experience and interpret literary works through dynamic, responsive visual elements.

Using p5.js and modern web technologies, these visualizations create immersive experiences that bridge the gap between text and visual art, allowing viewers to engage with poetry in new and meaningful ways.

Created with p5.js and TypeScript
